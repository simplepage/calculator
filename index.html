Calculator:
A calculator is a device that performs arithmetic operations on numbers.You input numbers and operations using the buttons, and it quickly calculates and displays the result on the screen.

Advantage of calculator:

  • Fast Math: Calculators help you do math quickly.
  • No Mistakes: They make sure your math is correct.
  • Lots of Functions: They can do lots of different types of math.
  • Help You Learn: They make learning math easier by doing the hard work for you.

Facilities in this calculator:

  • Addition
  • Subtraction
  • Multiplication
  • Division
  • Square
  • Square root
  • Factorial
  • logarithm
